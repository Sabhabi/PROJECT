About EcoShift

We connect buyers and sellers of Renewable Energy together

How we do this: From the clip below we can see the journey of Sally the Seller and Barry the Buyer. They are both connected through our platform which is a marketplace for renewable energy. As soon as Sally produces a surplus of renewable energy from her own energy system she offers it on our platform. Barry sees her offer and can instantly buy the electricity sold by Sally. Through connecting Sally and Barry directly together without a utility company in the middle, the market creates a much better margin for Sally. But it's not all about the price, it's about disrupting a conservative market and creating value for both sides, buyers and sellers.
